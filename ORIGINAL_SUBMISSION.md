## Idea
CivicOS AI – Your AI Copilot for Digital India
Government websites often have complex workflows, making it difficult for citizens to access essential public services. Many users, especially first-time users, senior citizens, and people with limited digital literacy, struggle to understand forms, navigation, and required documents.
CivicOS AI is an AI-powered browser assistant that acts as a digital copilot for government services. It opens the official website, understands the current webpage, and guides users step-by-step by highlighting the correct buttons, explaining forms in simple language, and answering questions in real time. As the user moves through the website, the assistant continuously analyzes each page and provides the next instruction. Optional voice support enables hands-free interaction for improved accessibility.
The system uses a Planner Agent to understand the user's goal, a Browser Agent to analyze webpages and identify interactive elements, a Vision Agent to read image-based content when needed, a Memory Agent to remember the current workflow locally without storing sensitive personal information, and a Safety Agent to ensure users remain in control of sensitive actions such as passwords, OTPs, and final submissions. Voice Agent support is optional and provides hands-free interaction.
The system follows a privacy-first approach by processing information locally whenever possible and never storing sensitive personal data. Instead of replacing government portals, CivicOS AI makes them easier to use, reducing confusion, saving time, minimizing errors, and making Digital India services more accessible for everyone.
